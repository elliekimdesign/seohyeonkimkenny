Earlier

Brand Manager at Benefit Cosmetics across 15 domestic duty-free locations in Korea. Worked on launching 20+ SKUs and running promotions in airport and downtown stores.

During one of our store visits at Incheon Airport, a woman and her teenage daughter walked in saying they still had about 40 minutes before boarding. They quickly scanned the store, tested only a couple of products, then naturally followed our visual cues to the gift-set corner and finished their purchase in a short amount of time. Watching scenes like this taught me how travelers actually behave under time pressure: they skim instead of read, follow sightlines instead of floor plans, and make decisions in seconds rather than minutes. That understanding now directly shapes how I design information architecture and user flows as a design engineer, so users can find their way through a digital product as intuitively as those passengers found their way through our duty-free store.
